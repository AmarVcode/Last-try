SK Robotics LLP is a designer, assembler, integrator, distributor and after sales service provider for the best in service robots globally with specific applications and use-cases in retail, software development, construction, bank, education, showrooms and hospitality.
SK Robotics LLP is a part of SK Group of Companies, having 40+ years lineage and history in performing global business operations in 82 countries worldwide having managed 1000+ strong workforce across 23 locations globally.
SK Robotics LLP is also an expert in implementing intelligent Service Robots, specially Humanoid Robots. Robots that require intelligent navigation systems, multi-operational synergies between cross operating platforms for eg. Android, Linux and Windows applications in one robot. SK Robotics LLP undertakes turnkey consultative projects, for end-to-end Robotics and Automation requirements. With an expert award winning team in Robotics, as mentioned below, you will receive a transparent quote from SK Robotics LLP right from enquiry to commissioning. Also, we will take payments, only after completing the project.
